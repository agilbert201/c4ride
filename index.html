Cabot Ride the Ridges

Cabot Ride the Ridges is a fun, challenging mostly dirt road bike ride through the scenic landscapes and rugged terrain of Cabot and Peacham, Vermont. All proceeds benefit the Cabot Connects Mentoring program. We will be offering 4 supported rides on September 14th, 2014.

The Rides

10K Family Fun Ride

The Sturdy 30K

60K Rugged Ridge Explorer

The Warped 100K
Because!

All rides begin at the Cabot town common at 8 a.m.

Apres

Apres ride includes Woodbelly wood fired pizza, a variety of Jasper Hill gourmet cheeses, the specialty goat cheeses of Vermont Creamery, Kingdom Creamery ice cream and Burtts Orchard apples.
