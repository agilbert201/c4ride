Support

These are all fully supported rides. Volunteers will man a number of aid stations along the route with snacks, power food and drinks. Note there will be a last time cutoff for the Peacham Aid station supporting our 100K riders.

Apres Ride

Post ride enjoy the best food Vermont has to offer, including Woodbelly wood fired pizza, a variety of Jasper Hill gourmet cheeses, the specialty goat cheeses of Vermont Creamery, Kingdom Creamery ice cream and Burtts Orchard apples.
