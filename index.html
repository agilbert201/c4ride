Support

These are all fully supported rides. Volunteers will man a number of aid stations along the route with snacks, power food and drinks. Including snacks from the likes of Nutty Stephs, Magic Spoon, Vermont Creamery, and Rhapsody Natural Foods.

Apres Ride + SWAG

Post ride enjoy the best food Vermont has to offer, including wood fired pizza, a variety of cheeses, ice cream and snacks all from likes of Jasper Hill, Vermont Creamery, and more. Plus swag and raffle awards from Hunger Mountain COOP, Goodrich's Maple Farm, Woodbury Mountain Toys, Kingdom Harvest Kambucha and Magic Spoon.
