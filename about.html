About Cabot Connects

For more than ten years, Cabot Connects has matched adult volunteers with young people (ages 9-17) to promote caring and supportive relationships that improve outcomes for youth and the community.

Cabot Connects is a tremendous success in many ways:

  • More than 30 adults are matched with youth in our community. Nearly one-quarter (24%) of eligible youth in Cabot have a mentor.
  • The Cabot Connects Mentor Program is greatly increasing the social competence and confidence of our youth, a vital factor to successful youth development, and has provided community members with a meaningful way to make a difference in their community.
  • Cabot Connects is the only year-round program that continues during the summer months and vacations, when youth often need additional support.
  • Cabot Connects has set a new precedent for collaboration in the community-engaging volunteers, advisory board members, businesses, organizations and institutions.
For further information please visit the Cabot Connects website.
